Moffett Food Service
Business Website

Moffett Food Service is one of the oldest family-owned businesses in Flint Michigan today; it is currently owned and run by the fifth generation of Moffett's. The Moffett family originated in Scotland. The founder, Samuel Moffett, arrived in Flint in 1865 when he was just nine years old.

AI Text Detection Tool
Python Project

I developed a lightweight neural network that detects whether text was written by a human or generative AI. Using a dataset of human essays and AI-generated samples, the system outputs a probability score indicating AI likelihood. While focused on educational use cases, the tool is adaptable for professional contexts such as business reports or journalism.
